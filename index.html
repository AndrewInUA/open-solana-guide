What is Solana?

Solana is public infrastructure for moving, holding, and using digital value – peer to peer, without a bank in the middle. Think of it as a shared ledger anyone can read, with rules enforced by code.

That opens a new layer of the internet where you can own digital things directly – money, art, tickets, identity – instead of renting access from a company.

Why learn this?

Not to chase trends – to understand money without banks, direct support for causes, digital creativity, and where identity may go next.
