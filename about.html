About this guide

Open Solana Guide is plain-English education for people who are new to Solana. It grew out of an earlier Ukrainian project (Solyanka) and is now written for an international audience – same calm tone, no pressure to invest.

Who built it

I am Andrii, known in the Solana community as AndrewInUA. I operate a Solana validator and build open tools for delegators – not as a traditional software developer, but as someone who cares about transparency, clear language, and bridges between complex systems and everyday users.

Before Solana I worked in journalism and communications. I joined the network in 2022 looking for resilient, meaningful infrastructure. This guide is one way I pay that forward: explain first, let you explore, let you decide.

What this is not

Not financial advice, not a promise of returns, not a substitute for doing your own checks. When you are ready to compare validators, use open metrics; when you stake, you remain in control of your wallet.
